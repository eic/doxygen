Detailed Description

[0] and [1] at positive

Bounds for a cylindrical Volume, the orientedSurfaces(..) method creates a vector of up to 6 surfaces:

case A) 3 Surfaces (full cylindrical tube): BoundarySurfaceFace [index]:

  • negativeFaceXY [0] : Acts::DiscSurface with , parallel to plane at negative
  • positiveFaceXY [1] : Acts::DiscSurface with , parallel to plane at positive
  • cylinderCover [2] : Acts::CylinderSurface confining the Acts::Volume

case B) 4 Surfaces (tube with inner and outer radius): BoundarySurfaceFace [index]:

  • negativeFaceXY [0] : Acts::DiscSurface with , parallel to plane at negative
  • positiveFaceXY [1] : Acts::DiscSurface with , parallel to plane at positive
  • tubeOuterCover [2] : Acts::CylinderSurface with
  • tubeInnerCover [3] : Acts::CylinderSurface with

case C) 6 Surfaces (sectoral tube with inner and outer radius): BoundarySurfaceFace [index]:

  • negativeFaceXY [0] : Acts::DiscSurface with and , parallel to plane at negative
  • positiveFaceXY [1] : Acts::DiscSurface with and , parallel to plane at positive
  • tubeSectorOuterCover [2] : Acts::CylinderSurface with
  • tubeSectorInnerCover [3] : Acts::CylinderSurface with
  • tubeSectorNegativePhi [4] : Rectangular Acts::PlaneSurface attached to [0] and [1] at negative
